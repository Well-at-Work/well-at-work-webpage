## 2. DET SKJULTE PROBLEM — "Hvorfor selv gode virksomheder overser det"

**Section intro:**
De fleste virksomheder gør det rigtige. Men trivselsdata giver dem et ufuldstændigt billede.

**Three cards:**

**a) "Hjernen ved det, før medarbejderen gør"**
Kognitiv performance — arbejdshukommelse, opmærksomhed, mental fleksibilitet — falder under mild stress, før medarbejderen selv oplever det. Spørgeskemaer kan kun fange det, man er bevidst om. Derfor opdages stress typisk for sent.

**b) "Ledere overvurderer trivslen — markant"**
Forskning viser en systematisk kløft på over 20 procentpoint mellem lederes vurdering af medarbejdernes trivsel og medarbejdernes egen oplevelse. Ikke af ond vilje — men fordi stress er usynlig, og kulturen holder den skjult.

**c) "Individuelle indsatser alene holder ikke"**
Mindfulness, resiliens-træning og stresshåndtering kan reducere symptomer på kort sigt — men effekten forsvinder typisk inden for 12 måneder, medmindre indsatsen kombineres med organisatoriske forandringer. Derfor giver fragmenterede løsninger svag ROI.

**Design note:** No source citations on the front page — keep it clean and authoritative. Each card can link to a full article on /forskning. Tone: empathetic, not accusatory. "Here's why even good organizations struggle" — not "here's what you're doing wrong."

## 4. EN ANDEN TILGANG — "Én samlet motor — ikke flere fragmenter"

**Headline:**
Én samlet motor — ikke endnu et værktøj

**Intro:**
De fleste organisationer har disconnected elementer: en APV, en trivselsmåling, en psykolog, en wellbeing-app, et lederkursus. Hvert element opererer på ufuldstændig information. Ingen vidensoverførsel. Ingen ved, hvad der faktisk virker.

Well at Work er den centrale motor der forbinder det hele:

**Four-step visual flow (connected nodes or horizontal pipeline):**

→ **Mål**
Subjektive spørgsmål + objektive kognitive tests. 300+ variable. 15 minutter.

→ **Forstå**
Tidlige mønstre, skjulte kognitive markører, motivation og personlighedsprofil.

→ **Handl**
Skræddersyede interventioner til medarbejder, team og organisation.

→ **Følg op**
Kvartalsvise målinger. Evaluér indsatser. Justér. Dokumentér effekt.

**Key line:**
Vi giver medarbejderen indsigt og konkrete øvelser. Og vi giver ledelsen det overblik og de anbefalinger, der gør det muligt at handle på det, der tæller mest.

**Design note:** This is the most important section strategically. The "central engine vs. fragments" positioning changes the competitive frame entirely. You're not another tool in the stack — you're the system that replaces the stack. The four steps show that measurement is only step one of four, which neutralizes the "so it's another survey?" objection. Consider using the app mockups (phones with brain character, questionnaire, emotion recognition) alongside this section — they're your strongest visual asset.

## /forskning — Videnskaben

NOT a generic "we're research-based" badge. A substantive, accessible science section.

**Core narrative: "Hvorfor spørgsmål ikke er nok"**
When you're stressed, your prefrontal cortex — working memory, attention, flexible thinking — starts underperforming before you consciously feel stressed. Traditional surveys only catch what you're aware of. Our cognitive tests measure how your brain actually performs, revealing patterns that self-report misses entirely.

**Full evidence sections (each can be its own article for SEO):**
1. "Din hjerne ved det, før du gør" — PFC and early detection (Qin 2009, Liston 2009, Dolcos, Lupien 1999)
2. "Hvorfor trivselsmålinger kan gøre mere skade end gavn" — Measurement reactivity and rumination (Conner & Lehman 2012)
3. "Ledere overvurderer trivslen — med over 20%" — The perception gap (McKinsey 2022, Gallup 2022, CIPD)
4. "Individuelle indsatser holder ikke alene" — Why combined interventions matter (MDPI 2024, JD-R model, Bakker & Demerouti)
5. "Det dobbelte afkast" — Same resources prevent burnout AND drive performance (JD-R dual pathway)

**Credibility signals:**
- Udviklet af professor i neurovidenskab med 20+ internationale priser
- Funderet i 20 års forskning fra Aarhus Universitet og University College London
- 1.100+ respondenter fra 30+ lande i valideringsstudier
- Teknologi fra teamet bag Cercare Medical (FDA-godkendt medicinsk AI) og Mindway AI (13 mio. brugere)

**Visual assets:**
- Radar chart comparison: "Samme WHO-score, forskellige profiler" (from deck — strongest proof of depth beyond self-report)
- Positioning matrix: actionable insight vs. intrusiveness (from deck)
- Dual measurement diagram: subjective + objective = complete picture

# STRATEGIC SUMMARY
# ═══════════════════════════════════════

**Tone:** Respectful, not provocative. "Your investment deserves better tools" — not "you're doing it wrong."

**Front page discipline:** 7 sections, one narrative arc: validate → problem → cost/opportunity → solution → proof → action. Everything else lives on subpages.

**Positioning:** "The central engine that replaces the fragmented approach" — not "a better survey tool."

**Business case:** Cost avoidance AND performance acceleration (JD-R dual pathway). Moved to position 3 so HR buyer sees it early enough to build internal case.

**Competitive frame:** You're not competing against other survey tools. You're replacing a broken, disconnected approach with an integrated system.

**Branding:** Use "Well at Work" consistently on all customer-facing material. If "Odin" continues as internal/tech brand, keep it off the website.

**Language:** Danish default. Add EN toggle in nav for EU expansion. Start by translating hero, approach, and pricing sections.
